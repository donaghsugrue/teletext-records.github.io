Welcome to



TELETEXT RECORDS



teletext records is an irish music management & promotion label with a focus on the more left of fieldwelcome to creative acts that also offer a different live concert experience.


Teletext Records operates on an open call submission basis and aims to also offer reviews of this submitted music, host showcase concerts and regularly release compilation records of our artists, with lots more planned ideas to be enacted in the near future.
